Getting Started

Welcome to HackFlow! This platform helps you manage hackathon queues, monitor crowd flow, and coordinate volunteers in real-time.

For Participants

  • Create an account or sign in with Google
  • Join a food queue from your dashboard
  • View your position in line
  • Get notified when it's your turn

For Volunteers

  • Apply to become a volunteer
  • Wait for admin approval
  • Manage food counters
  • Handle help requests
  • Monitor crowd zones

For Admins

  • Approve volunteer applications
  • Manage user roles
  • Control counter status
  • Send broadcasts
  • View analytics

Health endpoint: /api/health
